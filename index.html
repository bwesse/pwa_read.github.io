Chapter 1 • Level A2

The Harbor

You arrive at a small village by the sea. The air smells of salt and fresh bread. Fishermen talk quietly near their boats, and the water moves in slow, gentle waves.

A narrow street leads up the hill. There is a soft light in the windows above you, and somewhere a dog barks once, then falls silent again.

About

This is a tiny reading app prototype. It focuses on calm, distraction-free text. Later, it can grow into a full language-learning reading platform.

Idea

The idea is simple: graded stories, clean reading, and a smooth path to more complex language. The same core stories can live both here and in EPUB form.

More

Future additions might include multiple levels, inline vocabulary, audio, and synced progress across devices — all starting from this minimal base.
